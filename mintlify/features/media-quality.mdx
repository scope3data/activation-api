## Key Media Quality Signals

### Brand Safety & Content Classification
- Content category analysis and scoring
- Brand risk assessment algorithms
- Publisher reputation metrics
- Contextual safety verification

### Performance Predictors
- **Viewability Forecasting**: ML models predict viewability rates before bidding
- **Completion Rate Prediction**: Video completion likelihood based on placement context
- **Engagement Quality**: User interaction patterns and attention metrics
- **Ad-to-Content Ratio**: Content density analysis for optimal placement

### Inventory Quality Tiers
- Premium inventory identification and scoring
- Publisher direct relationship indicators  
- Fraud detection and prevention signals
- Real-time quality assessment
