# Agentic Sales Platforms

Sales agents are the bridge between your ad inventory and the agentic advertising ecosystem.

The following companies offer agentic sales platforms that are integrated into Scope3:

| Company                     | Supported Ad Servers |
| --------------------------- | -------------------- |
| [Swivel](https://swivel.ai) | Springserve, Freewheel |

If your preferred vendor isn't listed, please have them contact Scope3 to get set up!

<Info>
You may need to [integrate AXE](/mintlify/partners/axe-integration) to connect Scope3 to your ad server for real-time decisioning. Ask your ASP whether they handle this integration for you.
