Scope3's proprietary algorithms power intelligent campaign optimization, combining machine learning, signal processing, and performance prediction to maximize advertising effectiveness.

## Algorithm Suite

### INTELLIGENT_PMPS Optimization
- **Dynamic Bidding**: Real-time bid optimization based on inventory quality and performance predictions
- **Budget Allocation**: Intelligent budget distribution across tactics and inventory sources
- **Audience Discovery**: Automated identification of high-performing audience segments
- **Creative Optimization**: Performance-based creative rotation and testing

### Signal Processing Algorithms
- **Signal RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**: Intelligent signal combination for optimal targeting
- **Quality Scoring**: Media quality assessment and brand safety verification
- **Performance Prediction**: Viewability, completion rate, and conversion forecasting
- **Anomaly Detection**: Real-time identification of performance outliers and fraud

### Machine Learning Models
- **Deep Learning Networks**: Neural networks for pattern recognition in campaign performance
- **Reinforcement Learning**: Continuous optimization based on performance feedback
- **Natural Language Processing**: Campaign prompt interpretation and goal extraction
- **Computer Vision**: Creative analysis and performance optimization

- **Multi-Armed Bandit**: Exploration vs exploitation balance for optimal performance

- **Ensemble Methods**: Combination of multiple algorithms for robust performance

### Quality Assurance
- **Algorithm Validation**: Continuous testing and validation of algorithm performance
- **Performance Benchmarking**: Regular comparison against industry standards
- **Bias Detection**: Automated identification and correction of algorithmic bias
