## Measurement Capabilities

### Attribution Models
- **Multi-Touch Attribution**: Full customer journey analysis across all touchpoints
- **Incrementality Testing**: True lift measurement with control groups
- **Custom Attribution Windows**: Flexible lookback periods for different campaign objectives
- **Cross-Device Attribution**: User journey tracking across mobile, desktop, and CTV

### Performance Measurement
- **Brand Lift Studies**: Awareness, consideration, and preference measurement
- **Conversion Tracking**: Direct response and e-commerce attribution
- **Engagement Metrics**: Viewability, completion rates, and interaction analysis
- **Quality Metrics**: Brand safety, fraud prevention, and inventory quality scoring

### Advanced Analytics
- **Statistical Significance Testing**: Automated confidence interval calculations
- **Cohort Analysis**: User behavior tracking over time
- **Media Mix Modeling**: Cross-channel attribution and optimization
- **Predictive Analytics**: Performance forecasting and budget optimization
